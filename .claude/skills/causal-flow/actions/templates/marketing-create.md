# Marketing Content Creation Template

**Action Type:** `marketing:create`

## Objective
Create campaign content (copy, design, video) based on research insights.

## Pre-requisites
- Research completed (insights, themes, messaging)
- Brand guidelines available
- Content calendar slot identified

## Process

### 1. Content Planning (AI-assisted, 1 day)

**Content Brief:**
- Objective: {Awareness/Leads/Conversions}
- Target audience: {ICP segment}
- Key message: {Primary message from research}
- Content format: {LinkedIn post, blog article, email, video}
- Length: {Word count or duration}
- CTA: {Request demo, download whitepaper, sign up}

**Asset Checklist:**
- [ ] Copy (headline, body, CTA)
- [ ] Visuals (images, graphics, screenshots)
- [ ] Video (if applicable)
- [ ] Landing page (if campaign-specific)

### 2. Copy Creation (AI-drafted, 1-2 days)

**LinkedIn Posts (5 posts):**
- Post 1: Problem awareness (hook with pain point)
- Post 2: Solution introduction (product announcement)
- Post 3: Social proof (customer story, metrics)
- Post 4: Technical deep-dive (how it works)
- Post 5: Call-to-action (demo request)

**Blog Article (1 long-form):**
- Structure: Problem → Solution → Case Study → CTA
- Length: 1,200-1,800 words
- SEO optimized (target keywords from research)

**Email Campaign (3 emails):**
- Email 1: Announcement (product launch)
- Email 2: Value prop (ROI calculator, case study)
- Email 3: Urgency (limited time offer, demo slots filling)

**Copy Checklist:**
- [ ] Headlines (attention-grabbing)
- [ ] Body (clear, benefit-focused)
- [ ] CTAs (specific, action-oriented)
- [ ] Brand voice (consistent)

### 3. Visual Design (AI-assisted, 1-2 days)

**Graphics:**
- [ ] Featured images (blog, LinkedIn)
- [ ] Infographics (data visualization)
- [ ] Screenshots (product features)
- [ ] Social media cards (1200x630 for LinkedIn)

**Design Tools:**
- Canva (templates + AI design)
- Midjourney (custom imagery)
- Figma (brand assets)

**Brand Consistency:**
- [ ] Color palette (brand colors)
- [ ] Typography (brand fonts)
- [ ] Logo placement
- [ ] Style guidelines followed

### 4. Video Creation (AI-assisted, 2-3 days if needed)

**Video Script:**
- Hook (first 3 seconds)
- Problem (audience pain point)
- Solution (product demo)
- Social proof (customer quote)
- CTA (request demo)

**Video Production:**
- Screen recording (product demo)
- Voiceover (AI-generated or human)
- Captions (accessibility)
- Thumbnail (click-worthy)

**Length:**
- LinkedIn: 30-90 seconds
- YouTube: 2-5 minutes

### 5. Landing Page (AI-generated, 1 day if needed)

**Page Structure:**
- Hero: Headline + CTA
- Problem: Audience pain points
- Solution: Product benefits
- Social proof: Customer logos, testimonials
- CTA: Demo request form

**Conversion Optimization:**
- [ ] Clear value proposition
- [ ] Trust signals (logos, testimonials)
- [ ] Low-friction CTA (2-3 form fields)
- [ ] Mobile responsive

### 6. Content Review & Approval (Human, 1-2 days)

**Review Checklist:**
- [ ] Messaging aligned with research
- [ ] Brand voice consistent
- [ ] Grammar/spelling error-free
- [ ] Visuals high quality
- [ ] CTAs working (links tested)

**Approval Process:**
- [ ] First draft reviewed by owner (Bella)
- [ ] Revisions made (if needed)
- [ ] Final approval obtained
- [ ] Assets archived in content library

## Success Criteria
- [ ] All content assets created (copy, visuals, video)
- [ ] Brand guidelines followed
- [ ] Messaging aligned with research insights
- [ ] Human approval obtained
- [ ] Quality score ≥4/5 stars

## AI Automation

- Landing page generation (template + customization)

- **Approval decision** (go/no-go/revise)

## Typical Duration
**5-7 days** (draft + review + approval)

## Cost
**$1K-$3K** depending on complexity:
- Copy: $500-$1K (AI-drafted + human review)
- Visuals: $300-$800 (AI-generated + design time)
- Video: $500-$1K (if needed)
- Landing page: $300-$500 (if needed)

## Content Quality Standards

**Copy:**
- Clear, benefit-focused (not feature-focused)
- Conversational tone (not corporate jargon)
- Scannable (headers, bullets, short paragraphs)
- Error-free (grammar, spelling)

**Visuals:**
- High resolution (min 1200px wide)
- Brand consistent (colors, fonts, style)
- Accessible (alt text, captions)
- Mobile-optimized

**Video:**
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Captions (80% watch on mute)
- Clear CTA at end
- Thumbnail optimized for click

## Red Flags
- Generic copy (not specific to audience)
- Off-brand design (wrong colors, fonts)
- Weak CTA (vague, low urgency)
- No social proof (missing credibility)
- Mobile broken (not responsive)

## Green Flags
- Audience-specific language (uses their words)
- Strong hook (grabs attention)
- Clear value proposition (obvious benefit)
- Social proof included (logos, testimonials)
- CTA tested and working

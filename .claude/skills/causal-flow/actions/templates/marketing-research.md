# Marketing Research Template

**Action Type:** `marketing:research`

## Objective
Gather audience insights, competitive intelligence, and market trends to inform campaign strategy.

## Pre-requisites
- Campaign goal defined (awareness, leads, conversions)
- Target audience identified (ICP)

## Process

### 1. Audience Research (AI-automated, 1-2 days)

**Demographics:**
- Job titles, seniority levels
- Company size, industry, revenue
- Geographic location
- Technology stack (if B2B)

**Psychographics:**
- Pain points and challenges
- Content consumption habits (LinkedIn, blogs, podcasts)
- Buying triggers
- Decision criteria

**Data Sources:**
- LinkedIn audience insights
- CRM data (existing customers)
- Website analytics (visitor behavior)
- Social listening tools

### 2. Competitive Analysis (AI-automated)

**Competitor Content:**
- What topics are they covering?
- What formats (blog, video, LinkedIn posts)?
- Engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares)
- Messaging themes

**Gaps & Opportunities:**
- Underserved topics
- Better angles on existing topics
- Differentiation opportunities

**Tools:**
- SpyFu, SEMrush (SEO/content)
- Social listening (Hootsuite, Brandwatch)
- Manual review (LinkedIn, competitor blogs)

### 3. Trend Analysis (AI-automated)

**Market Trends:**
- Google Trends (search volume)
- LinkedIn trending topics
- Industry news and events
- Regulatory changes (if applicable)

**Seasonal Patterns:**
- Budget cycles (B2B: Q1/Q4)
- Industry events (conferences, product launches)
- Holiday impacts

### 4. Keyword Research (AI-automated, if SEO)

**Primary Keywords:**
- Search volume, competition
- Intent (informational, commercial, transactional)

**Long-tail Keywords:**
- Lower competition, higher intent

**Tools:**
- Ahrefs, SEMrush
- Google Keyword Planner

### 5. Insights Synthesis (AI-automated)

**Research Report:**
- 3-5 key insights (actionable findings)
- Content themes (what to focus on)
- Channel mix (where to publish)
- Messaging framework (how to position)

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Audience profile completed (demographics + psychographics)
- [ ] Competitive gaps identified (3+ opportunities)
- [ ] Trend analysis completed
- [ ] 3-5 actionable insights documented

## AI Automation
- **Full automation** (audience research, competitive analysis, trend analysis, insights synthesis)

## Human Touch Points
- None (review optional)

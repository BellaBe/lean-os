# Marketing Publish Template

**Action Type:** `marketing:publish`

## Objective
Publish approved content to target channels.

## Process (AI-automated, 1 day)

1. **Schedule Content**
   - LinkedIn: Posts scheduled (Buffer/Hootsuite)
   - Blog: Article published to CMS
   - Email: Campaign loaded to ESP (Mailchimp)
   - Website: Landing page deployed

2. **SEO Optimization**
   - Meta tags (title, description)
   - Alt text (images)
   - Internal links
   - Schema markup

3. **Tracking Setup**
   - UTM codes (source, medium, campaign)
   - Conversion pixels
   - Analytics events

## Success Criteria
- [ ] All content published on schedule
- [ ] Tracking codes working
- [ ] Links tested

## Output Format
```markdown
# Publish: {Campaign Name}
**Date:** {YYYY-MM-DD}

## Published Assets
- LinkedIn: {5 posts scheduled}
- Blog: {URL}
- Email: {campaign name, sent to {count} subscribers}
- Landing page: {URL}

## Tracking
- UTM campaign: {name}
- Conversion goal: {demo_request}
```

## Email Templates

### Template 1: Curiosity Open

Best for: Newsletters, product launches, content promotion

```
SUBJECT: [Information Gap]
"The [unexpected thing] that [surprising outcome]..."
"Why [common belief] is wrong (and what to do instead)"
"I shouldn't share this, but..."
→ Bias: Information Gap + Zeigarnik

OPENING: [Pattern Interrupt]
"[Unexpected statement or question]"
NOT: "I hope this email finds you well"
→ Bias: Von Restorff (distinctiveness)

BODY: [Concrete + Loop]
"[Specific story or data point]
[Build curiosity]
[Reveal partial insight]
The full [answer/method/solution]? [CTA to learn more]"
→ Bias: Concrete Language + Zeigarnik + Information Gap

CTA: [Single + Clear]
"[Action verb] → [Link]"
→ Bias: Friction Reduction

P.S.: [Scarcity or Social Proof]
"P.S. [X] people already [action]. [Urgency if real]."
→ Bias: Social Proof + Scarcity
```

### Template 2: Loss Frame Email

Best for: Re-engagement, urgency campaigns, abandoned cart

```
SUBJECT: [Loss Frame]
"You're about to lose [specific thing]"
"Your [benefit] expires in [time]"
→ Bias: Loss Aversion + Scarcity

OPENING: [Direct Loss Statement]
"[Name], your [specific benefit] is about to disappear."
→ Bias: Loss Aversion + Personalization

BODY: [Quantified Loss]
"Without [product/offer], you'll:
- Miss out on [specific benefit with number]
- Continue losing [specific cost]
- Fall behind [benchmark]"
→ Bias: Loss Aversion + Concrete Language

REVERSAL: [Easy Solution]
"But it doesn't have to be this way.
[One simple action] and you're back on track."
→ Bias: Friction Reduction

CTA: [Urgent + Single]
"[Reclaim/Keep/Restore] [Benefit] →"
"[Time remaining indicator]"
→ Bias: Loss Aversion + Scarcity

P.S.: [Deadline]
"P.S. This expires [specific date/time]. After that, [consequence]."
→ Bias: Scarcity + Loss Aversion
```

### Template 3: Social Proof Email

Best for: Trust building, testimonial features, case studies

```
SUBJECT: [Specific Result]
"How [Customer] got [specific result] in [timeframe]"
"[X]% increase in [metric] — here's how"
→ Bias: Social Proof + Concrete Language

OPENING: [Relatable Problem]
"[Customer Name] was struggling with [problem you solve].
Sound familiar?"
→ Bias: Social Proof + Pattern matching

BODY: [Story Arc]
"Before: [Specific situation with numbers]
The moment: [Decision to try your solution]
After: [Specific results with numbers]

Here's exactly what [Customer] did:
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]"
→ Bias: Concrete Language + Operational Transparency

BRIDGE: [Reader Application]
"You could see similar results.
[Specific reason why their situation applies]"
→ Bias: Social Proof transfer

CTA: [Low Friction]
"See if it works for you →"
"[Risk reversal: trial/guarantee]"
→ Bias: Friction Reduction
```

---

### Template 4: Value-First Email

Best for: Nurture sequences, relationship building, reciprocity plays

```
SUBJECT: [Gift/Value Signal]
"[Resource] for you (no strings)"
"Thought this might help with [their problem]"
→ Bias: Reciprocity

OPENING: [Context]
"I noticed [something specific about them/their situation]."
→ Bias: Personalization + Research signal

BODY: [Pure Value]
"Here's [resource/insight/template] that helped [others like them]:
[Actual valuable content or link]

Use it however you like — no catch."
→ Bias: Reciprocity (give without asking)

SOFT CLOSE: [Optional Bridge]
"If you ever want to chat about [topic], I'm around.
Either way, hope this helps."
→ Bias: Low pressure + Reciprocity setup

NO HARD CTA in this email — the ask comes later.
```

## Headlines Formula Bank

### Loss Frame Headlines

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| "Stop Losing [Amount] to [Problem]" | "Stop Losing $400/month to Manual Reporting" |
| "The Hidden [Problem] Costing You [Amount] Every [Period]" | "The Hidden Bug Costing You 23% of Signups Every Week" |
| "[X]% of [Audience] Make This [Amount] Mistake" | "78% of SaaS Founders Make This $50K Mistake" |
| "What [Not Doing X] Really Costs You" | "What Skipping User Research Really Costs You" |
| "You're Losing [X] Every [Period] — Here's Why" | "You're Losing 5 Hours Every Week — Here's Why" |

### Curiosity Headlines

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| "The [Unexpected Thing] Behind [Outcome]" | "The Counterintuitive Tactic Behind Stripe's Growth" |
| "Why [Common Belief] Is Wrong" | "Why 'More Features' Is Wrong for Enterprise Sales" |
| "What [Authority/Company] Knows About [Topic] That You Don't" | "What Netflix Knows About Retention That You Don't" |
| "The [Adjective] Secret to [Outcome]" | "The Boring Secret to 10x Pipeline" |
| "[Number] [Things] Nobody Tells You About [Topic]" | "5 Things Nobody Tells You About Founder Sales" |

### Concrete Outcome Headlines

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| "[Outcome] in [Timeframe]: The [Method] That Works" | "2x Demo Bookings in 30 Days: The Method That Works" |
| "How to [Achieve X] in [Time] (Step-by-Step)" | "How to Close Enterprise Deals in 45 Days (Step-by-Step)" |
| "Get [Specific Result] Without [Common Pain]" | "Get 50 Qualified Leads Without Cold Calling" |
| "[Number] [Things] to [Outcome] [Timeframe]" | "3 Changes to Double Conversions This Week" |
| "From [Before State] to [After State] in [Time]" | "From 2% to 8% Conversion in 60 Days" |

### Social Proof Headlines

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| "How [X,XXX] [Audience] [Achieved Outcome]" | "How 2,847 B2B Teams Doubled Their Pipeline" |
| "[Customer] Went From [Before] to [After] in [Time]" | "Acme Went From $0 to $2M ARR in 8 Months" |
| "The [Method] Used by [Impressive Companies/People]" | "The Sales Process Used by Stripe, Notion, and Linear" |
| "Why [Number] [Audience] Switched to [Product]" | "Why 500+ SaaS Companies Switched to [Product]" |
| "Join [X] [Audience] Who [Outcome]" | "Join 12,000 Marketers Who Ship Faster" |

### Rhyming Headlines (Keats Heuristic)

Use sparingly — only when it fits naturally:

| Formula | Example |
|---------|---------|
| "[Verb] to [Rhyme]" | "Click to Ship" / "Post and Coast" |
| "[Benefit] Without the [Rhyming Pain]" | "Growth Without the Guesswork" |
| "[Action], [Outcome]" | "Set and Forget" / "Plan, Then Land" |

**Test:** Rhyming version vs non-rhyming. Rhymes often +17% perceived truth.

## CTA Button Text

### High Commitment (when value is clear)

| CTA | When to Use |
|-----|-------------|
| "Start My Free Trial" | Self-serve, clear product value |
| "Get My [Outcome]" | Outcome-focused products |
| "Claim My [Benefit]" | Offer-based, scarcity present |
| "Create My Account" | Clear next step, low friction |
| "Begin My [Transformation]" | Courses, coaching, change-focused |

### Low Commitment (trust-building)

| CTA | When to Use |
|-----|-------------|
| "See How It Works" | Complex products, skeptical audience |
| "Learn More" | Educational, awareness stage |
| "Show Me" | Demo-focused, visual products |
| "Explore [Product]" | Self-serve discovery |
| "Watch the Demo" | Video-first approach |

### Urgency CTAs (when scarcity is real)

| CTA | When to Use |
|-----|-------------|
| "Claim Before [Deadline]" | Time-limited offer |
| "Get [X] Remaining Spots" | Quantity scarcity |
| "Lock In [Benefit]" | Price increase coming |
| "Reserve My Spot" | Events, cohorts, limited access |
| "Join Before Doors Close" | Launch, enrollment period |

### Loss Frame CTAs

| CTA | When to Use |
|-----|-------------|
| "Stop Losing [Thing]" | Problem-focused products |
| "Reclaim My [Benefit]" | Re-engagement, recovery |
| "Fix This Now" | Urgent problems |
| "Protect My [Asset]" | Security, insurance, backup |
| "Don't Miss Out" | FOMO (use sparingly) |

### Action-Specific CTAs

| CTA | When to Use |
|-----|-------------|
| "Download Now" | Lead magnets, resources |
| "Get the [Resource]" | Specific deliverable |
| "Book My Call" | Sales-assisted |
| "Send Me the Guide" | Email capture |
| "Calculate My [Metric]" | Interactive tools |

## Template Selection Guide

| Situation | Template | Primary Biases |
|-----------|----------|----------------|
| Product launch | Curiosity Email + BAB Landing | Information Gap, Scarcity |
| Conversion optimization | Loss Frame Email + PAS Landing | Loss Aversion, Friction |
| Trust building | Social Proof Email | Messenger Effect, Concrete |
| Re-engagement | Loss Frame Email | Loss Aversion, Scarcity |
| Nurture sequence | Value-First Email | Reciprocity |
| Brand awareness | Curiosity Headlines | Von Restorff, Concrete |
| Price-sensitive audience | Concrete Outcome Headlines | Temporal Reframe, Anchoring |
| Skeptical audience | Social Proof Headlines | Messenger Effect, Specificity |

## Bias Slot Quick Reference

When filling template slots, use this mapping:

| Slot Type | Best Biases |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Headlines** | Loss Aversion, Curiosity, Concrete, Distinctiveness |
| **Subheads** | Social Proof, Authority, Specificity |
| **Problem sections** | Loss Aversion, Concrete Language |
| **Solution sections** | Effort Transparency, Operational Transparency |
| **Proof sections** | Social Proof, Messenger Effect |
| **Pricing sections** | Anchoring, Decoy, Temporal Reframe |
| **CTAs** | Friction Reduction, Scarcity, Present Bias |
| **P.S. lines** | Scarcity, Social Proof, Loss Aversion |

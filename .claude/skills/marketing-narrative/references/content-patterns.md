# Content Patterns

All patterns follow this structure.

## Pattern Template

```markdown
# Pattern: {Content Type}

## Purpose
{What this content achieves}

## When to Use
- {trigger/situation}
- {trigger/situation}

## Structure

### Opening
{How to start  -  hook, problem, or insight}

### Body
{Core structure  -  sections, flow, key elements}

### Closing
{How to end  -  takeaway, next step}

## Length
{Target length or range}

{How brand voice applies specifically to this pattern}

## Examples

{Example content demonstrating the pattern}

### Example 2: {Title}
{Example content demonstrating the pattern}
```

---

## Default Patterns

### Insight

**Purpose:** Share observation or opinion that demonstrates expertise

**Structure:**
- Hook: Counter-intuitive observation
- Body: Evidence, experience, implications
- Close: Reframe or call to reflection

**Length:** 300-600 words (LinkedIn), 800-1500 words (blog)

### Tutorial

**Purpose:** Teach a specific skill or method

**Structure:**
- Hook: Problem or outcome
- Body: Step-by-step with examples
- Close: Summary and next steps

**Length:** 500-1000 words (LinkedIn), 1500-2500 words (blog)

### Case Study

**Purpose:** Demonstrate results through customer story

**Structure:**
- Hook: Result headline
- Body: Challenge → Solution → Results
- Close: Testimonial and CTA

**Length:** 400-800 words (LinkedIn), 1000-2000 words (blog)

### Story

**Purpose:** Build connection through narrative

**Structure:**
- Hook: Moment of tension or change
- Body: Journey, struggle, realization
- Close: Lesson or transformation

**Length:** 300-700 words (LinkedIn), 800-1500 words (blog)

# Risk Action Skill

## Purpose
Identify and prioritize risks.

## When to use
- Risk assessment
- Threat modeling
- Pre-mortems

Do NOT use for:
- execution plans (use planning-action)

## Operating rules
1. Define scope and horizon.
2. Identify risks across categories.
3. Score likelihood and impact.
4. Propose high-level mitigations only.

## Outputs
### Risk register
### Top risks
### Mitigation themes
### Open questions
